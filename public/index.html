What is SYNAPSE?

SYNAPSE is a free PYQ portal built for SPPU engineering students. Find previous year question papers organized by year, branch, and subject — no registration, no paywalls.

  • ✦ Free to access and download
  • ✦ Organized by Year → Branch → Subject
  • ✦ PDFs directly from SPPU exams
  • ✦ Fast search across all papers
