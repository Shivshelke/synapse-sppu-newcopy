Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Answers to common questions about Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) Engineering exams and PYQs.

Where can I find SPPU Engineering Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs)?

You can find them right here on SYNAPSE. We offer a comprehensive database of question papers for Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) Engineering courses, completely organized by branch and academic year.

Which engineering branches are supported?

We host PYQs for all major SPPU branches including Computer Engineering, Information Technology (IT), Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication (E&TC), and Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI-DS).

Are these question papers updated for the 2024 Pattern?

Yes, all the resources and question papers available on the SYNAPSE portal are updated specifically for the latest SPPU 2024 Pattern, covering First Year (FE), Second Year (SE), Third Year (TE), and Fourth Year (BE).

Does SYNAPSE provide In-Sem and End-Sem question papers?

Absolutely. We organize both In-Semester (In-Sem) and End-Semester (End-Sem) exam papers for Pune University classes, making it simple to study for whichever exam session is coming up next.
